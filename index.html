    FAQYour question our answers

    Our appliances use the UV-C spectrum of UV light to eliminate pathogens that include the COVID -19 causing Corona Virus.

    Many a research has shown that UV-C light has germicidal effects and is has shown to kill numerous pathogens including the SARS, MERS, of viruses. The COVID-19 virus comes under this category of viruses and is technically classified as SARS COV 2.

    Following is an extract from a research paper of germicidal effects of UV light on pathogens (Ref https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3925713/ )

    …UVC light: wavelength range lies between 200 and 280 nm; this electromagnetic spectrum has biocidal effects and generally is reported as “germicidal” or more usually “ultraviolet germicidal irradiation” (UVGI)…

    … Energetically UVC is very important in the context of inactivation of microorganisms, since UVC directly affects deoxyribonucleic acid (DNA) and ribonucleic acid (RNA) by inducing molecular transformation (i.e., producing photoproducts in the genetic material) …

    … Due to its killing effects on microorganisms, other applications of the UVC have been extended into the food processing industry, disinfecting heating–cooling coils, ventilating and air-conditioning systems, whole room/surface disinfection, and into killing of all human pathogens (bacterial, viral, and protozoan) transmitted via water …

    The kill property of the appliance is based upon the following variables

    • Power of the UV-C source
    • The distance of the object from the source
    • The type of object
    Our product data sheets have relevant time of exposure data for your reference. Use this data to effectively eliminate the virus from the surfaces.

    It is true that UV-C has adverse effects on human skin if there is prolonged exposure, but our appliances have safety features such as delay timers and restrictive openings that help in eliminating exposure to the operators. You should at all times:

    1. Leave the room when using the UV-C Room Sanitizer.
    2. Not look directly at the UV Lamp in the UV-C Hand Held Wand whilst operating.
    3. Not put any organic, live matter such as pets in the UV -C Cabinet.
    4. Only the operator should be present when using any of the appliances.
    5. THE APPLIANCE IS NOT A TOY – KEEP IT SAFE FROM CHILDEREN AND PETS AT ALL TIMES

    The system uses a surface disinfectant authorised by the WHO in a water-soluble form.
    This solution is delivered in a high-pressure mist within a tunnel structure.
    The mist is formed of droplets of approximately 150 microns.

    We use a very low percentage of Sodium Hypo Chlorite as suggested by the WHO for surface disinfection. The concentration is based upon a recent study by the National chemical Laboratories in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

    We recommend the following steps to maximise decontamination

    • When entering the tunnel try and keep your eyes and nose covered. (closing your eyes will be sufficient). The system operator will direct you.
    • Walk slowly with arms raised to your head level
    • When in the centre make a full turn
    • Walk out of the tunnel
    • Only use the tunnel one person at a time
      • We do not recommend the use of the tunnel for the following persons
    • Small children
    • People with skin disorders such as Eczema, Psoriasis, etc.
    • People who are allergic to the solution used (Sodium Hypo Chlorite or one used as per local regulations)
    • Pregnant and breastfeeding women
